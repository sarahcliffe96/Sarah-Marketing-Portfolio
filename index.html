Clamp4Fire Product Relaunch

The Challenge

Clamp4Fire had been on the market for several years but had gained very little traction despite solving a genuine installation problem for electricians.

Strategy

I developed a multi-channel marketing strategy designed to educate installers and increase awareness within the electrical industry.

Execution

  • Influencer marketing collaboration with industry content creators
  • Product demonstration videos showing real installation scenarios
  • Trade show demonstrations with live product testing
  • Targeted marketing to wholesalers and contractors

Results

  • 367,000+ video views
  • Major increase in product awareness
  • Significant improvement in sales performance

Fix4Fire Campaign

The Challenge

Fix4Fire was a technically strong product but required clearer positioning within the electrical safety market.

Strategy

Focus messaging on compliance, safety, and installer convenience while leveraging both digital and trade channels.

Execution

  • Educational product content explaining fire safety regulations
  • Distributor marketing support
  • Installation demonstrations and product comparisons

Results

  • Improved installer awareness
  • Increased distributor engagement
  • Stronger product positioning within the range

Trade Show Product Display Strategy

The Challenge

Trade show displays often fail to communicate product value quickly enough to busy attendees.

Strategy

Design visually engaging display boxes that clearly demonstrate the installation and benefits of the product.

Execution

  • Created modular product display boxes
  • Used real installation examples
  • Designed layouts highlighting product benefits

Results

  • Improved engagement at trade shows
  • Clearer product understanding for visitors
  • Increased sales conversations during events
