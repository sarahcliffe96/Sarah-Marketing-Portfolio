Case Study: Product Relaunch

The Clamp4Fire cable clamp had been part of the Olympic Fixings product range for several years but had generated little to no sales, despite clear potential within the electrical installation market. I identified the product as a strong candidate for relaunch, targeting both electrical wholesaler partners and electricians using the product on-site.

My Role

  • Influencer marketing partnerships
  • Trade media campaigns
  • Video content production
  • Exhibition product promotion
  • Distributor awareness campaigns

Strategy

The campaign focused on influencer partnerships, educational trade media content and supporting the campaign through industry magazines and exhibitions.

Results

  • 367,000 video views across influencer content
  • 9,300 views on long‑form educational demonstration video
  • Product became a Top 20 best‑selling item from 4,000 SKUs
  • Significant increase in industry awareness
